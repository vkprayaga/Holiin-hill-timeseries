GPS surveys at peg locations

column 1 = date
columns [2-4] = x, y, z location (in British National Grid coordinate) of peg 1 at specific date
columns [5-7] = x, y, z location (in British National Grid coordinate) of peg 2 at specific date
...

File pegs_movement_diff_2015-2019.csv
column 1 = date
columns [2-4] = relative displacement along x, y, z axes in metres for peg 1 at specific date
columns [5-7] = relative displacement along x, y, z axes in metres for peg 1 at specific date
...
x, y, z axes are here (E-W, N-S, and elevation)
